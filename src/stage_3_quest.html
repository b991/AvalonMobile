Stage 3: Quest
You have debated well and wisely chosen the brave knights and ladies with whom you place your trust. Now it is time to measure a person's true intent and loyalty to the noble cause for which Arthur fights.
The selected knights and ladies are embarking on their quest. The whole Avalon is waiting for their success.
Tap card title to go on quest.
Make sure to tap again to hide your selection.
Note: The Good players must select the Quest Suces card; Evil may select either hte Quest Suces or Quest Fail card.
Note: The 4th Quest (and only the 4th Quest) in games of 7 or more players requires at least two Quest Fail cards to be a failed Quest.
